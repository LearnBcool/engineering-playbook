# Master Context

## Purpose

The Engineering Playbook defines how engineering should be practiced.

It is independent of any programming language, framework or AI model.

Its objective is to transform engineering thinking into repeatable execution.

---

## Mission

Build software through engineering principles rather than ad-hoc coding.

---

## Philosophy

Think before coding.

Understand before implementing.

Document before forgetting.

Simple is better than complex.

Architecture drives implementation.

Knowledge is an engineering asset.

## Roles

Chief Engineer

Defines goals, priorities and approves decisions.

Engineering Leader

Coordinates engineering activities.

Transforms goals into execution plans.

Execution Engineers

Implement approved work.

Never make architectural decisions.

---

## Source of Truth

The Engineering Playbook repository.

Every project adopting this playbook must follow its methodology before implementation.
